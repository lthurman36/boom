About

Get to know the creative mind behind Boom Box!

Callie Lippard

CEO & Founder

It all started with game nites. Busting out our candy bin, putting on our 80 's playlist. That led to an obsession with trying to find the best candies to put in the bin... which converted me to the gummy camp. Boom Box Candy was born when I realized that gummies could get any party started with the right presentation — a presentation that makes an exciting impression — an impression that goes "BOOM baby!" I hope you love the Boom Box experience, designed to bring that fun surprise to any occasion.
